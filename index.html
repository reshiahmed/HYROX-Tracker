What is HYROX?

A fixed-format fitness race.

Every HYROX race uses the same structure worldwide: run 1 km, complete the next workout station, then repeat until all 8 runs and 8 stations are finished. That fixed order makes it easy to train, compare splits, and know exactly what is coming next.

What this app does

Pick your division and run a full practice checklist.

Use it during training to move through the race in order, time each section, keep total elapsed time, track completion, and save a share image when the session is done. It works as an installable PWA and caches the core app for bad-signal gyms.
